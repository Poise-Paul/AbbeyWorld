About Us

Learn More About Us

Abbeyworld is an exciting and unique Community Service Program that is designed to engage and inspire. Since 2000, we have provided numerous participants with engaging experiences that they take with them for the rest of their lives. We provide links to available services within the community. We help people get information as quickly as they need it. We have weblinks to over 100 government-funded services within the London community
